Current Scenario

In a family psychiatric and/or addiction problem comes as a curse and for those who have less understanding and lack of manpower, this problem becomes a fatal problem. In a nut-shell each and every family having mentally ill member needs extra support, care, treatment, medicine, guidance; rehabilitation & re-organization for their ward & to some extent for themselves also.

Requirement of Rehabilitation

The situation becomes worst where there is no care taker is available, lack of facilities, family members or siblings are not interested in taking care for long duration, nuclear families and some other kind of such situations. Even after giving best available medicines why results are not measurable? Does family give them medicines in time? Does family give them support and care of the required extent? Does family give quality times to them? These are some questions which can be solved by providing required expertise professional special care and rehabilitation assistance to the mentally ill & addicted persons and their family member. We cater every solution that persist regarding this and a lot more pertaining to these. We hope to give you the best with what we have.
